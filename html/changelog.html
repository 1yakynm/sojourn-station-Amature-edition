08 April 2020

APWill and Vixen updated:

  • Added warning decals, new equipment, and other QoL upgrades to the scrap beacon.
  • Changed the southern Church dorms into open space to streamline the map. Wasn't needed.
  • Various bugfixes and enhancements for the biogenerator and biosorter.
  • Fixed turrets trying to attack drones, despite being on the same side. Traitors.

    • 07 April 2020

    Kazkin and Heedless Defiance updated:

    • New sprites for varying lengths of ammunition magazine, and some armor vests.
    • Various improvements and optimizations to the combat and general weapon systems.
    • Fixed a massive number of minor map errors and inconsistencies.

      • 06 April 2020

      Tenno and Heedless Defiance updated:

      • Added sounds to grenade launchers.
      • Added Security ammunition disks.
      • Added an armored Blackshield Militia overcoat. This will be implemented on the map shortly.
      • Fixed some ammunition not loading into guns it should have done.

      04 April 2020

      Kazkin and Heedless Defiance updated:

      • Added our own lobby screen!
      • Added a timer to restart votes - with a minimum of three hours.
      • The Entertainment Studio has been moved to a central location near the bar, with a stage added.
      • The Sunken Nightclub was moved to an undisclosed location.
      • Adjusted calibers of all firearms - standardized to a few universal sizes.
      • Changed and added some firearm-related sprites.
      • Added some of the modified weaponry to the armory.
      • Improved the equipment available in the Janitor closet, and gave the role cargo access.
      • Corpsmen now have chemistry access, so they do not have to break in when no doctors are present.
      • Rangers now have brig access, since they have the most authority over it barring heads.
      • Trooper and Officer now have 4 job slots. Corpsmen and Rangers now have 2 job slots.
      • Primal Renders now have double speed. Please run faster.
      • Mimics have updated sprites, damage, and speed - but reduced health. There is also a way to spot them now...
      • Fixed some box, backpack, broken tile, document, and lipstick sprites not loading properly.
      • Various map fixes including better wiring, piping, tile/object placement, and lighting.
      • Fixed a loadout error relating to crowbars.
      • Fixed an issue with CRO and CBO not being able to select their specific loadout items.
      • Fixed an issue with changing colonist access.
      • Cyborgs now spawn properly in the storage closets and can log out via the same machine that spawns them in.
      • Nanopaste had research points reduced to prevent an exploit.
      • Fixed those in the Prospector department not showing up properly on the manifest.
      • Removed a faulty research disc that caused a conflict.
      • Fixed an issue with delivery chutes draining atmospherics.
      • Fixed an issue with some machines disappearing when powered off.
      • Removed some items from the church bioprinter public disk that should not have been there.
      • Various other minor bugs and fixes. Thanks for all the reports!
